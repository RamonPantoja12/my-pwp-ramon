Content Strategy for PWP Milestone Two

Navbar:

My navigation tab will include my name and links to every feature of my website. Links to my about me, Portfolio, and contact information will be provided. It will also be a sticky navbar.

Home page:

My Homepage will include my name and role as the title along with an animated background image that will fade when the user scrolls down

About me:

The about me section will include a professional portrait photo of myself followed by a detailed description of my experience skill sets such as tools and languages that i've learned.

Portfolio:

In this section of my personal website i will provide a few examples of past projects that i have done including a few projects that i have worked on prior and during the bootcamp. This will be an animated gallery with a link that will direct the user to the respective project.

Contact:

This will mainly focus on my social media or ways to get in contact with me via email. Links to my social media will also be included on my footer with their respective icons.
